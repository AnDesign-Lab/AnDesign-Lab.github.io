Who We Are

StrumUp is a community club created for teens who want to learn guitar in a relaxed, supportive environment. We meet weekly to practice chords, learn songs, and jam together.

Why Join StrumUp?

  • Meet other teens who love music.
  • Learn guitar at your own pace.
  • Get guidance from more experienced players.
  • Perform at small, low-pressure events.

We welcome beginners and experienced players. You only need curiosity and a willingness to practice.
