Getting Started with Guitar

Here are some basic skills we focus on in our beginner sessions.

Beginner Skills

  • Essential chords
    • G major
    • C major
    • D major
  • Strumming patterns
    • Downstrokes on the beat
    • Down-up patterns for simple songs
  • Rhythm and timing
    • Counting 1–2–3–4
    • Playing along with a metronome

Short Practice Video

The video below introduces absolute beginners to their first guitar chords, demonstrating how to hold the instrument, form basic shapes, and strum smoothly at a comfortable pace.

Accessible Description

In this video, a teen guitarist slowly switches between G, C, and D chords while counting out loud: “one, two, three, four.” The camera focuses on the fretting hand so viewers can see finger placement clearly. The instructor reminds players to press firmly on the strings and to change chords right on the beat.
