Beginner-Friendly Gear

You do not need expensive equipment to start playing guitar. Here are some simple suggestions.

Recommended Gear Table

Beginner Gear Comparison
Item Type Price Range Beginner-Friendly Notes
Acoustic Guitar A Acoustic $100–$150 Yes Comfortable neck, good for small hands.
Electric Guitar B Electric $150–$250 Yes Requires small practice amp.
Clip-on Tuner Accessory $10–$20 Yes Makes tuning fast and easy.
Guitar Picks Pack Accessory $5–$10 Yes Try different thicknesses to see what you like.
